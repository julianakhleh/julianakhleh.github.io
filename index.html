Julia Nakhleh

jnakhleh@lanl.gov · jnakhleh@asu.edu

I'm a post-bachelor's research assistant at Los Alamos National Laboratory (LANL) working on machine learning for scientific analysis and high performance computing. I got my B.S. in Computer Science from Arizona State University in 2019. I did my undergraduate honors thesis on deep learning for generalized planning with Dr. Siddharth Srivastava's Autonomous Agents and Intelligent Robotics (AAIR) lab at ASU.

My research interests are in machine learning theory and algorithm development for applications including (but not limited to) scientific analysis, optimization, high-performance computing, and natural language processing.
